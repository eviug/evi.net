Enlight Vocational Institute

Enlight Vocational Institute (EVI) was founded in 2021 under Enlight Educational Institutions. EVI is a skills-based training institution dedicated to equipping learners with practical knowledge for the modern job market. We focus on hands-on learning, ensuring that every student gains real experience in their chosen field. Our programs empower learners with the ability to secure employment or start their own businesses.

Core Values

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Discipline, Teamwork, and Lifelong Learning.

Vision

To be a leading vocational training institute recognized for excellence in practical skills development and empowerment.

Mission

To provide accessible, affordable, and high-quality vocational training that equips learners with relevant skills.
