Who We Are

Enlight Vocational Institute is a modern vocational training institution located in Matugga, Wakiso District along the Kampala–Gulu Highway. The institute is committed to providing quality hands-on education that prepares students for real-world careers and entrepreneurship opportunities. Our learning environment combines practical training, innovation, discipline, and creativity to help students become competent professionals and future leaders.

Our Mission

To provide industry-relevant vocational education that equips learners with practical skills, innovation, and professionalism for employment and self-reliance.

Our Vision

To become a leading center of excellence in vocational and technical education, producing skilled graduates who transform communities and industries.

Objectives

Excellence, Integrity, Innovation, Discipline, Teamwork, and Lifelong Learning.

About

Enlight Vocational Institute (EVI) was founded in 2021 under Enlight Educational Institutions. EVI is a skills-based training institution fully accredited and recognized by the Ministry of Education and the National Council for Higher Education.
