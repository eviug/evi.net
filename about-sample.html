About Us

Enlight Vocational Institute is dedicated to providing practical, career-oriented education that prepares learners for employment, entrepreneurship, and lifelong success. We combine quality teaching, hands-on training, and professional values to develop competent and innovative graduates.

Our History

Founded to bridge the gap between education and industry demands, Enlight Vocational Institute has grown into a respected center for vocational excellence. Through modern training methods and a learner-centered approach, we continue to equip students with practical skills that improve lives and communities.

Our Mission

To provide accessible, high-quality vocational education and practical training that empowers learners with skills, knowledge, and values necessary for employment, entrepreneurship, and community development.

Our Vision

To be a leading vocational training institution recognized for excellence in skills development, innovation, and producing competent professionals who contribute positively to society.
